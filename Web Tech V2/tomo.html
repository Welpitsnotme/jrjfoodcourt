Authentic Japanese Delicacies

The flavors were incredibly authentic and reminded me of home. The Kaisendon (Seafood Bowl) was fresh and bursting with flavors, while the Tonkotsu Ramen had a rich, creamy broth that was absolutely perfect. The atmosphere was serene, and the staff were very attentive and polite.

Food: 5/5 | Service: 5/5 | Atmosphere: 5/5

Recommended dishes: Seafood Bowl (Kaisendon), Tonkotsu Ramen, Tempura Soba

Great Food and Cozy Atmosphere

The Gyudon (Beef Bowl) was well-prepared with tender beef slices, and the Spicy Miso Ramen had the right amount of heat. I also appreciated the Cold Soba (Zaru Soba), which was refreshing and light, perfect for a warm day. The service was a bit slow during peak hours, but the food made up for it.

Food: 4/5 | Service: 3/5 | Atmosphere: 5/5

Recommended dishes: Beef Bowl (Gyudon), Spicy Miso Ramen, Cold Soba (Zaru Soba)

Great Food with Traditional Flavors

The Beef Noodle Soup Rice was rich and hearty, with tender beef slices and perfectly cooked rice. The Tomato Egg (Fan Qie Chao Dan Fan) was simple yet comforting, just like how my grandmother used to make it. The atmosphere was cozy, though it was a bit crowded during peak hours.

Food: 4/5 | Service: 4/5 | Atmosphere: 3/5

Recommended dishes: Beef Noodle Soup Rice, Tomato Egg, Scallion Pancake
